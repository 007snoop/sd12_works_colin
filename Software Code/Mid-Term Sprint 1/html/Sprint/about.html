Meet The Dungeon Master

I'm DM Celestine (AKA Colin), guiding the adventures of our brave party. I'm passionate about crafting immersive stories and challenging quests for our players. I've also created a table for others to use if they want.

D20 Roll Encounter Type Description
1-2 Skrimish

A gang of thieves attempts to rob a local merchant right in front of the players. Will they intervene or stand by?

3-4 Informant

A cloaked figure slips a note to the party, offering valuable information about the Cursed Ruby in exchange for a favor.

5-6 Political

A city official mistakes the party for hired mercenaries and tries to recruit them to spy on a rival.

7-8 Social

The party is invited to a lavish party by a wealthy noble who has interests in ancient artifacts, including the ruby.

9-10 Street Festival

The party encounters a street festival where locals share tales of Waterdeep's legends, possibly including clues about the ruby.

11-12 Crime Scene

The city watch is investigating a murder that seems to involve arcane forces. The party could offer their aid.

13-14 Magcial Mishap

A botched spell from a novice wizard causes chaos in the market square, releasing a swarm of magical frogs.

15-16 Underworld Deal

The party overhears a secretive deal about the Cursed Ruby between shadowy figures in a dark alley.

17-18 Chase

The party witnesses a thief stealing a valuable item; a chase through the busy streets of Waterdeep ensues.

19-20 Discovery

The party finds a hidden passage leading to an ancient part of the city, untouched by time and possibly holding secrets of the past.
